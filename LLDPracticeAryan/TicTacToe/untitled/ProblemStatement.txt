Low Level Design of Tic Tac Toe game

Tic Tac Toe also known as Xs and Os is 2-player game played on a 3x3 grid

Objective:- While the rules are simple, devising an unbeatable strategy requires a keen understanding of the game's dynamics

Rules of the game :-
    1) The game is played on 3x3 grid
    2) One player uses X and the other uses O
    3) Players take turns making their moves

 Winner :-

    1. The game is won by the player who places their symbol in a row, column or diagonal
    2. The first player to get three symbols in a row wins the game
    3. Once this condition is met, the game is ended immediately

 Draw :-

        1. If all grid cells are filled and no player has three symbols in a row, the game ends in a tie.


 Illegal moves :-

 A player cannot:-

    1) Place a symbol on a tile already occupied by an opponent's symbol.
    2) Place their symbol on a tile they already own
    3) Move to a non-empty cell -  the move must be made to an empty space.
